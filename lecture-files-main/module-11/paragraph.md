Across the five-day bootcamp, participants move from writing their first
prompt with Gemini to shipping a small offline AI app that runs entirely on
their own laptop. The first day is about responsible AI, the agent-ready
repository, and the RTCF prompt framework. The second day turns the same
tools onto documents, datasets, and a quick Stitch prototype. The third day
is about structured decision support: a one-page executive brief, a SWOT and
risk matrix, and a backlog an AI agent can read. The fourth day takes the
cloud out of the loop: students install Ollama or LM Studio, run Gemma 4 on
their own hardware, build a small CLI app against the local endpoint, and
design a workflow with explicit risk and governance controls. The fifth day
is the Shark Tank capstone, where teams pitch a real AI solution to a real
organisation.
